UiPath Amazon Price Monitoring Bot

UiPath Studio UiPath Orchestrator Excel SMTP / Outlook Automation Data Scraping Exception Handling & Logging

Project Overview

A UiPath bot was designed and deployed to automatically scrape real-time product data from Amazon every 30 minutes, ensuring up-to-date insights on prices, availability, and reviews. The bot processes and cleans the data, triggers timely email alerts for significant changes, and generates dynamic reports to support smarter pricing decisions. Leveraging UiPath Orchestrator, the solution delivers continuous automation with high reliability and improved operational efficiency.

Data Overview

The dataset consists of real-time product information scraped from Amazon, including price, stock availability, customer reviews, and ratings. Data is captured every 30 minutes to track dynamic changes, enabling timely detection of price fluctuations, stock updates, and new listings. This structured and cleaned dataset supports detailed trend analysis and personalized alert generation.
